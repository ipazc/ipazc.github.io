Summary Iván de Paz Centeno is a computer scientist from Spain, holding a Ph.D. in Computer Science with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence. He has made significant scientific contributions to the field of Artificial Intelligence. Currently based in León, Castilla y León, Spain, Iván has a robust background in both industry and academia. His expertise lies in developing Artificial Intelligence models for various applications, focusing on energy time series analysis, computer vision, and machine learning. He is a dedicated learner, problem solver, and collaborator, known for delivering impactful solutions.
